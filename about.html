About Us

Welcome to Little Paws

Welcome to Little Paws whose moto is to help stray animals from human cruelity.
Little Paws is a non profitable Organization who help injured stray animals. Our organization have tieup with NGO who works on animal welfare.We both together treat needy animals and give them new life.

In our busy lives, we never give a second thought to the little creatures, such as cats and dogs, wandering around our neighbourhood. We consider it routine and nothing of significance.Some times we see them injured on road or in our neighbour we also feel bad about them but because of our busy schedule or some limitations we can't help them and we forget about it next moment.What we forget is that, we, as humans, have a responsibility to help, care and pay attention to the needy and helpless creatures who depend on us. And this is what the moto of our organization. You can help them by simply notifying us about needy animals by registering them on our website and thus you can fulfill your responsibility as a human on earth. We will also appreciate your good work by uploading your photo and name on our website, so that more people are attracted to help strays.

How It Works

When someone uploads the details of injured animals. We contact our NGO partner and send them important information about that animal. And they send their rescue team to that locality and bring them to our treatment center. We provide all possible help to them and give free treatment from best Vats available in that city. Along with that we also take care of them and feed them until they will recover completely.

We're helping those who can't help themselves.

Animals are sentient, intelligent, perceptive, funny and entertaining. We owe them a duty of care as we do to children
